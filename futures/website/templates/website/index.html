Welcome to the Futures Contracts Marketplace




Market participants (players) include:

  • Institutions
  • Farmers
  • Agro Industries (Manufucturers)
  • Retailers (Almost expired/Expired)
  • Individuals.. Peer to Peer


Our Goals for “Afect the Cause” 2019 Hackathon

  • Reduce Food waste
  • Dispose of waste responsibly
  • Recycle waste
  • Create a sustainable environment
  • Provide a guarantee for the purchase of by-products at a (current / future / Agreed upon ) price / date

Benefits of this system

  • Avail working capital
  • Increase/Improve the market and market accessibility
  • Hedge risk
  • Central place for inputs and outputs
  • Good vibes for the environment: | Manage food waste | Recycle food waste | Use waste and inputs in other industries | Donate food to needy & non profits | Make Profit

Types of market place transactions

  • bid on item auction style.
  • open a contract on an item (guarantee to purchase item at future date)
  • open a contract on an item (purchase item at CURRENT price for guarantee to receive item at future date)
  • open a contract on an item (purchase item at AGREED price for guarantee to receive item at future date)
